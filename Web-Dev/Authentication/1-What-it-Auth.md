# What is Authentication?

- It is a process to verify you

- Makes the application secure

- Offline analogy:

    - Show your ID card at office gate to enter

## Difference between Authentication & Authorization:

- **Authentication** is to verify <span style="color: #0f0">**who you are**</span>

- **Authorization** is to verify <span style="color: #0f0">**what you can access**</span>
